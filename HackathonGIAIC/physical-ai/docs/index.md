# Physical AI

## Building Intelligent Systems That Interact with the Real World

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI. This book is designed for beginner-to-intermediate developers who want to understand how AI systems perceive, decide, and act in physical environments.

Dive into the complete spectrum of Physical AI, from foundational concepts to advanced integration techniques:
