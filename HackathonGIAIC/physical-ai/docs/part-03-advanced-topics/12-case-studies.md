## Case Study 1: Autonomous Delivery Robots

### System Overview
Small wheeled platform for urban package delivery

### Key Components
- **Perception**: Vision and LIDAR for navigation
- **Localization**: SLAM in urban environments
- **Planning**: Route planning with dynamic obstacles
- **Manipulation**: Package detection and handoff
- **Safety**: Multiple fail-safe mechanisms

### Challenges Encountered
- GPS unreliability in urban canyons
- Pedestrian interaction and safety
- Weather robustness
- Real-time constraints

### Lessons Learned
- Simplicity and robustness beat complexity
- Testing in real environments is essential
- Human-robot interaction requires careful design

## Case Study 2: Industrial Robot Manipulation

### System Overview
Collaborative robot performing assembly tasks

### Key Components
- **Manipulation**: Precise arm control
- **Vision**: Quality inspection
- **Safety**: Force-limiting for human collaboration
- **Adaptation**: Learning from experience

### Challenges
- Maintaining precision over long runs
- Adapting to component variations
- Human safety in shared workspace

### Lessons Learned
- Hardware reliability is critical
- Simple algorithms often outperform complex ones
- Human operators are essential

## Case Study 3: Autonomous Agricultural Systems

### System Overview
Autonomous tractor system for crop management

### Key Components
- **Perception**: Crop health monitoring
- **Navigation**: GPS-based and visual
- **Actuation**: Spray, mow, or harvest
- **Learning**: Adapt to field conditions

### Challenges
- Outdoor robustness to weather
- Energy constraints in large-scale operation
- Integration with existing farm practices
